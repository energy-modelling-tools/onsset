Open-Source Spatial Electrification tool (OnSSET)

OnSSET is a GIS-based optimization tool developed to support electrification planning and decision making for achieving energy access goals in currently unserved locations. The tool‚Äôs flexible and modular codebase allows customization of analysis resolution, GIS input data, technology types and costs, and electrification policies as needed.

OnSSET is a dynamic open-source project: improvements are integrated in real time and guided by a steering committee. Membership and contributions are open to everyone.
